AI journal and writing memory

Write it down. Let AI remember with care.

Ink & Memory is an AI journal and writing memory tool for reflective writers. It combines daily journaling, writing timelines, long-term memory, memory-based chat, AI feedback, voice notes, and consent-based editing in one private workspace.

What is Ink & Memory?

Ink & Memory preserves diary entries, dreams, fragments, essays, and voice notes as autobiographical context. The assistant can use that context to offer prompts, comments, rewrites, and structured reflection without taking control of the document.

Why consent-based AI writing matters

Before rewriting, deleting, inserting, or replying inside a document, the assistant explains the action, scope, rationale, risk, and before-and-after text. The writer approves or rejects the change.

Who is it for?

  • Journalers who write diaries, dreams, affective notes, essays, or creative fragments.
  • Self-explorers who want AI to remember context without forcing a diagnosis.
  • Writers who want contextual prompts and edits while keeping final authority over the text.
